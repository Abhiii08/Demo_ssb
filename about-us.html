ABOUT US

Shree Siraj Buildcon: A Trusted Leader in Construction

Founded in 2007 by visionary entrepreneurs Mr. Ganesh Pohane and Mr. Santosh Tote, Shree Siraj Buildcon has established itself as a key name in the construction industry. With an impressive track record of delivering excellence, the company has successfully completed projects making a total of 5,5 lakh square feet. Shree Siraj Buildcon specializes in providing high-quality contracting services that bring significant value to residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects. Leveraging years of expertise and an unwavering commitment to precision and quality, the company has built a reputation for reliability and innovation. With a focus on quality and customer satisfaction, Shree Siraj Buildcon is dedicated to delivering projects that meet the unique needs of its clients. From residential complexes to commercial spaces and industrial facilities, the company consistently provides reliable and high-quality construction services.
